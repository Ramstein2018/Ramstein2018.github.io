FAQ: USCIS Green Card Immigration Physical Exam

Our green card exam covers a complete medical evaluation required by USCIS, including:

Medical Exam

Comprehensive physical examination to assess your overall health.

Vaccinations

Required vaccines are administered as part of the preventative care.

Paper Filing of Form I-693

We handle the completion and submission of your Form I-693, which is essential for your green card application. The completed form is provided to you in a sealed envelope.

Scanned Copy

You receive a scanned copy of the Form I-693 for your records.

Medical Exam and Form I-693 Filing

$350. This fee covers the medical exam and the preparation and filing of Form I-693, handed to you in a sealed envelope.

Labs

Although most lab tests are typically covered by insurance, if you are paying out of pocket, lab costs are approximately $250 through our office.

Vaccinations

Included in our services as part of preventative care. If paying out of pocket, each vaccine ranges from $60 to $150. General insurance often covers the cost of vaccines.

We prioritize efficiency and aim to complete your green card exam and paperwork swiftly. Typically, the entire process is completed in a single visit, ensuring you receive the necessary documentation in a timely manner.

Please bring the following to your appointment:

  • Valid photo ID (e.g., driver's license or passport).
  • Any previous vaccination records, if available.
  • Health insurance information, if applicable.

To schedule your green card exam, contact our office at 213-582-8527 (21-FLU-ATLAS) or contact us on our website. We offer flexible appointment times to accommodate your schedule.

During the exam, our experienced medical team will conduct a thorough physical examination, review your medical history, and administer any necessary vaccinations. Our goal is to ensure you meet all USCIS health requirements while providing compassionate and efficient care.

We accept various forms of payment, including cash, credit/debit cards, and checks. Payment details will be provided when you schedule your appointment.

After your exam, we will prepare and seal your Form I-693, which you will need to submit with your green card application. You will also receive a scanned copy for your records. If any additional follow-up is needed, we will inform you promptly.
